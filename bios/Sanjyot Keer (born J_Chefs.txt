Sanjyot Keer (born June 16, 1992) is an Indian chef and Internet personality, based in Mumbai, India. He was the food producer for TV competitive cooking show, MasterChef India (season 4), aired on Star Plus. Keer was featured in the list of Forbes India's Tycoons of Tomorrow 2018. He is the founder of Your Food Lab (YFL). He gained recognition for posting cooking videos to Facebook and YouTube, which accumulated millions of views on his content.
